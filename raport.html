1. Introduction:

1.1. Abstract:

The project is a web application that will allow the users to visualize informations regarding publications from a particular area or from a specific author. The informations will be gathered from "Google Scholar" or "Scopus" and saved in a data base.

2. Description:

2.1. Front-end:

The project will contain multiple web pages divided into two categories depending on the person that tries to access them : regular user and professor/admin. Including an autentification page.

2.1.1. Regular users:

Have access to the main page where they can search different articles/publications from a particular area or from a specific author.

2.1.2. Professors:

Besides the main page they have access to a different page where they can view dates regarding the number of citations for every publication. They will receive a score based on the number of citations.

2.1.3. Admins:

Besides the privilegies granted to the proffesors, they have access to the data base and they can make changes on it. They are also responsibles for the proffesors's accounts.

2.2. Back-end:

3. Personas

  • Regular user: John, 19 - wants to search informations for his homework at artificial intelligence.
  • Regular user: Jack, 20 - search the previous work of his proffesor.
  • Proffesor: Alice, 47 - wants to view the number of citations of her article.
  • Admin: Emil, 35 - updates the information from the data base and makes a new account for proffesor Mark.

4. Conclusion:

This application is designed to evaluate each proffesor with a grade based on the number of citations associated to his publications.
